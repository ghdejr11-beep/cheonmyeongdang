📚 Why Korean Tarot?

Traditional Western Tarot uses Greek elements (Fire/Water/Earth/Air). Korean Tarot reinterprets each card through Korean Five Elements (오행): 木 Wood (growth), 火 Fire (passion), 土 Earth (stability), 金 Metal (precision), 水 Water (wisdom).

Each card is also paired with a Saju (Four Pillars) reading insight — the heavenly stem and earthly branch context that gives a uniquely Korean perspective on Western archetypes.
