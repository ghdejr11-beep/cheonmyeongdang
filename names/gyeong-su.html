Gyeong-su

경수
한자: 慶秀
Gender
male
Hangul
경수
Hanja
慶秀
Pronunciation
GYUNG-soo (GYUNG like 'young' with a 'g', soo like 'sue')
Popularity
stable

Meaning

경(慶) means joy, congratulations, and a cause for celebration; 수(秀) means excellent, exceptional, and standing out beautifully above all others. Together they describe a person whose very excellence is a reason for celebration—someone whose achievements bring joy to all who know them.

Famous Examples: EXO D.O. (Do Kyung-soo); famous actor and singer with this name

Saju (Four Pillars) Reading

Fire and Wood elements; celebratory excellence that blooms and grows with natural vitality
