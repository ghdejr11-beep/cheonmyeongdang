Hye-rin

혜린
한자: 慧麟
Gender
female
Hangul
혜린
Hanja
慧麟
Pronunciation
HYE-rin (HYE like 'hye' in 'hyena', rin like 'rean')
Popularity
classic-revival

Meaning

혜(慧) means wisdom, intelligence, and spiritual insight; 린(麟) refers to the qilin—a mythical creature of good fortune, grace, and the arrival of a great person. Together they describe a woman of extraordinary wisdom with an almost mythical, otherworldly presence.

Personality Traits

wiseextraordinarygraceful

Cultural Context

K-Pop Match: EXID Hani (witty, charismatic, uniquely captivating)

Famous Examples: Actress Kim Hye-rin; popular in 1990s and returning now as a sophisticated choice

Saju (Four Pillars) Reading

Wood and Metal elements; insightful, rare, and exceptionally gifted
