Meaning

성(聖) means holy, sacred, and of the highest moral order—the sagehood of Confucian tradition; 현(賢) means wise, virtuous, and morally excellent. Together these two powerful virtue characters describe someone destined to be a sage—a person of exemplary wisdom and character.
