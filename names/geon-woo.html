Meaning

건(健) means strong, healthy, and vigorous in body and spirit; 우(宇) means universe and infinite cosmic space. Together they describe a man of extraordinary strength whose influence extends outward like the universe itself—boundless and powerful.

Famous Examples: Actor Ji Geon-woo; popular name in Korean action dramas
