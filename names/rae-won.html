Rae-won

래원
한자: 來苑
Gender
male
Hangul
래원
Hanja
來苑
Pronunciation
RAY-won (RAY like the word 'ray', won like 'one')

Meaning

래(來) means to come, to arrive, and to bring forth; 원(苑) means a cultivated garden or gathering place of beauty. Together they evoke a person whose presence transforms any space into a flourishing garden, and whose arrival is always welcomed.
