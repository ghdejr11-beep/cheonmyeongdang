📚 What Are Solar Terms (절기)?

Originating in ancient China and adapted into Korean tradition, the 24 절기 divide the solar year into ~15-day periods. Each marks specific agricultural and seasonal events — when to plant, harvest, eat certain foods, perform ancestral rites.

Modern uses: 입춘 (Spring Begins) is used for Saju year boundary calculations. 동지 (Winter Solstice) traditionally marks new year in some Korean rural traditions.
