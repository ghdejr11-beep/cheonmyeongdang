📚 Korean Wedding Date Tradition (길일)

길일 (Gilil) means "auspicious day" in Korean. Traditional Korean weddings follow Saju (사주, Four Pillars of Destiny) compatibility between bride and groom, plus the day's heavenly stem and earthly branch.

Avoided dates: 흑일 (black days), 손 없는 날 conflicts, ancestor death anniversaries, certain lunar phases.

Preferred dates: when bride/groom Saju elements harmonize with the day's Five Elements (Wood/Fire/Earth/Metal/Water).
