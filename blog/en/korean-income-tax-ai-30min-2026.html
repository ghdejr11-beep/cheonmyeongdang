Who needs to file 종합소득세?

You need to file Korean Comprehensive Income Tax if:

  • You're a Korean tax resident (183+ days in Korea OR domicile here), AND
  • You have income other than salary alone (freelance, business, rental, multi-source).

Salary-only employees: handled through 연말정산 (year-end settlement) in February — no May filing needed.
Everyone else: May 31 deadline. Miss it = 20% non-filing penalty + late payment interest.

A. Yes, if you're a Korean tax resident. The interface is currently Korean-first; English UI is on the roadmap. For foreign-engineer 5-year reduction or treaty benefits, we recommend an English-speaking CPA.

Q. Who's responsible if AI makes a mistake?
A. Filings reviewed by AI + optional CPA review fall under our penalty-coverage policy. Excludes cases where the user submitted incomplete or incorrect documents.

Q. Data security?
A. Official NTS API (MyData format) + immediate document deletion + end-to-end HTTPS. BillGate PG is PCI-DSS certified.

Q. What if my refund deposit is delayed?
A. NTS refunds within ~30 days of filing. Our 9.9% fee is charged only after refund deposits — never upfront.
