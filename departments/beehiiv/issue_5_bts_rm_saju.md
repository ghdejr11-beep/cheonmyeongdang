## What Yang Earth Means

Yang Earth (戊) is one of the 10 possible Day Masters. The classical metaphor is **a mountain** — broad, stable, slow to change, structurally protective of what stands on it.

Day-Master archetypes:

- **Personality:** steady, anchoring, protective of community, slow to react but immovable when committed
- **Strengths:** trust-building, holding center for groups under pressure, long-arc thinking
- **Tendencies:** can be perceived as inscrutable; needs deliberate channels for emotional output
- **Natural fit:** roles requiring sustained leadership of collectives — community builders, long-tenure leaders, cultural anchors

For a 7-member group's leader holding the center for over a decade, Yang Earth is exactly the Day Master profile that fits. The mountain holds the platform. People stand on it.

## The Ten Gods Read

Now we run each of the other 5 known characters against Yang Earth and assign Ten Gods roles.

- **Year Stem 갑 (Yang Wood)** → **편관 (Indirect Officer)** for Yang Earth. Translates as: external authority and pressure shaping early life. The world demanded something hard from this chart from the start.
- **Year Branch 술 (Dog, Earth)** → **비견 (Peer)**. Strong same-element peer. Translates as: deep group identity, brotherhood / sisterhood patterns, collective identity over individual.
- **Month Stem 계 (Yin Water)** → **정재 (Direct Wealth)**. Stable, methodical wealth pattern in the career-pillar position. Translates as: long-term professional accumulation through sustained craft, not speculation.
- **Month Branch 유 (Rooster, Metal)** → **상관 (Hurting Officer / Output)**. The output star in the career-pillar branch. Translates as: creative expression as the engine of the career. Words. Voice. Public articulation.
- **Day Branch 신 (Monkey, Metal)** → **식신 (Eating God / Output)**. Another output-star in the self-pillar. Translates as: expression isn't just the career — it's *who you are*.

Six known characters, and the dominant pattern is **doubled-up Output gods** (식상) sitting on a **Yang Earth foundation** with strong **Peer support** in the year pillar.

In Saju vocabulary, this is the chart of someone whose nature is to **produce expression from a stable, communal base**, with **structured pressure shaping it** since childhood.

You don't have to be a fan to recognize how cleanly this reads.
