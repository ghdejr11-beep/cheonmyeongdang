## What Is Saju, Really?

The literal translation of **Saju (사주, 四柱)** is **"four pillars."** Each pillar represents a column of time in your birth moment:

- **Year Pillar** — your ancestral and generational signature
- **Month Pillar** — your career and social personality
- **Day Pillar** — your authentic self and your romantic compatibility
- **Hour Pillar** — your hidden tendencies and late-life direction

Each pillar is built from **two characters**: one **Heavenly Stem** (천간) and one **Earthly Branch** (지지). That gives you **eight characters total**, which is why Saju is also called **BaZi (八字, "eight characters")** in Chinese tradition.

Where Western astrology gives you **one sign** for the sun, Saju gives you **eight interlocking codes** mapped against the **five elements** — Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water — and their interactions of nourishment, control, and conflict.

That's why Saju readings feel so specific. It isn't a horoscope. It's closer to a personality and timing engine.
