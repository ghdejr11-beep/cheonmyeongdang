## The Three Harmonies (삼합)

The Three Harmonies are **four trios** of zodiac animals that fuse into a powerful elemental energy when paired. Couples sharing a harmony cluster tend to feel **understood without explaining themselves**.

### Water Harmony — Rat · Dragon · Monkey
Logical, ambitious, status-aware. They build empires together. Watch out for: workaholism with no off switch.

### Fire Harmony — Tiger · Horse · Dog
Loyal, intense, passionate. They fight hard and reconcile harder. Watch out for: drama spirals when both partners are hurt at once.

### Wood Harmony — Pig · Rabbit · Goat
Gentle, artistic, harmony-seeking. The most "peaceful" trio. Watch out for: avoiding hard conversations until resentment builds.

### Metal Harmony — Snake · Rooster · Ox
Disciplined, principled, long-term. They love through reliability. Watch out for: emotional distance disguised as "respect for space."

If you and your partner are both inside the same trio, the relationship has a **structural tailwind**. You won't have to explain why you see the world a certain way — you both just do.

## The Six Clashes (육충)

The Six Clashes are **six pairs** of animals positioned 180 degrees apart on the zodiac wheel. The clash isn't a curse — it's a **direct opposition of operating styles**.

- **Rat ↔ Horse** — strategist vs. performer
- **Ox ↔ Goat** — builder vs. craftsman
- **Tiger ↔ Monkey** — founder vs. hacker
- **Rabbit ↔ Rooster** — diplomat vs. operator
- **Dragon ↔ Dog** — visionary vs. guardian
- **Snake ↔ Pig** — analyst vs. connector

Couples in a clash pair **don't fail more often** — they fail differently. The relationship becomes either **a forge or a war**, depending on whether both partners learn the other's logic.

In our reading data, clash pairs that **survive five years** often go on to be the most resilient marriages we see, because they've already metabolized opposition.

## What Most Western Compatibility Tests Miss

Western zodiac compatibility usually stops at sun sign × sun sign. Saju adds two crucial layers:

### 1. Day Pillar Compatibility (일주 궁합)
The **Day Pillar** is your authentic self in private — the version of you that shows up only after dating for six months. Day Pillar compatibility is **more predictive than year animal compatibility** for long-term relationships.

### 2. Element Balance Across Both Charts
A couple where one partner has *too much Fire* and the other has *plenty of Water* is often a **healing pair** — the elemental imbalance in one chart is corrected by the other. This is why opposites genuinely attract in some pairings and clash in others.

## Reader Question

**"My partner and I are in a clash pair. Should we worry?"**

No. The clash pair pattern only **predicts the type of friction**, not the outcome. The single biggest factor we see is whether **both partners can name the clash** without taking it personally. If you can say *"of course you do that, you're a Rooster — and of course I do this, I'm a Rabbit"*, the clash becomes a feature.

The only clash pattern that worries us is when **both partners refuse to learn the other's framework**. Then the friction never gets converted into trust.
