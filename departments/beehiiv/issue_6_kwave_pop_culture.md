## Why It Travels Better Than Other Eastern Systems

Vedic astrology has tried to cross over for decades. Chinese Bazi has the strongest practitioner tradition. Japanese Suimei (四柱推命) is the same root system. So why is **Korean** Saju the one breaking through globally?

Three structural reasons:

1. **K-Wave brand halo.** Anything labeled "Korean" carries instant cultural credibility for Gen-Z audiences right now. Korean Saju benefits from the same brand pull as K-drama and K-beauty.
2. **Living practitioner culture.** Saju in Korea isn't a museum tradition — it's mainstream, used at weddings, exams, baby names, business launches. That makes it feel alive in a way ancient-only systems don't.
3. **Pre-existing apps.** Korea has dozens of consumer Saju apps. The product layer has been refined for 20 years inside Korea. When global audiences arrive, the user experience is already polished.

Western astrology had Co-Star. Korean Saju has an entire ecosystem ready for export.
