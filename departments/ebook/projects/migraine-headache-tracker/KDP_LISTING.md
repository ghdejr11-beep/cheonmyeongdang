## Description (back cover / KDP A+)
Most patients walk into a neurologist's office trying to remember the last three months from memory. This 13-week tracker fixes that.

Each daily page captures peak pain (0-10), type and location, aura, associated symptoms (nausea, photophobia, allodynia), triggers (food, sleep, hormonal, weather, stress), rescue medication taken, and what actually relieved the attack. Weekly Reviews surface trends with a daily pain chart. Monthly Neurologist Visit Prep pages turn three months of data into a focused conversation with your doctor.

What's inside:
- 13 weeks of daily attack logs (0-10 severity scale, headache type, location, aura)
- 13 weekly review pages with daily pain trend graph
- 3 Neurologist Visit Prep sheets (top triggers, medication response, questions to ask, plan)
- Quick-reference trigger guide (food/drink, sleep, hormonal, environment, weather, stress, physical, medical)
- Preventive & rescue medication log with medication-overuse headache (MOH) alert
- Personal info, headache history, primary diagnosis, allergies

Designed for chronic migraine sufferers, tension headache patients, cluster headache fighters, and anyone whose neurologist asked them to "keep a headache diary." Identify triggers most patients miss. Walk into your next appointment with receipts.
