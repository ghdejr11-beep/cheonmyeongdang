## Description (back cover / KDP A+)
Perimenopause symptoms can shift week to week — and most women walk into doctor's appointments trying to remember the last three months from memory. This 14-week tracker fixes that.

Each daily page captures hot flash count, sleep, mood, energy, brain fog, joint pain, cycle changes, HRT and supplement doses, and possible triggers. Weekly Reviews surface trends. Monthly Doctor Visit Prep pages turn three months of data into a clear conversation with your gynecologist.

What's inside:
- 14 weeks of daily symptom logs (0-5 severity scale, 10 core symptoms)
- 14 weekly review pages with hot flash trend graph
- 3 Doctor Visit Prep sheets (top symptoms, questions to ask, plan)
- Quick-reference symptom guide (vasomotor, sleep, mood, cognitive, cycle, body, skin & hair, genitourinary, energy)
- HRT & supplement log with dose / route / start date
- Personal info & medical history page

Designed for women in perimenopause (typically 35-55) who want real data, not guesswork — and who want to walk into their next appointment prepared.
