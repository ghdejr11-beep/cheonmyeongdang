**WHY THIS JOURNAL**
Apps are great for fast counts, but paper teaches observation. Sketching a bird's posture, writing out a song in syllables, and noting habitat and behavior in your own hand sharpen the kind of attention that turns "sparrow" into "white-throated sparrow" — and lights up a lifetime hobby. This journal is light enough for a field bag, structured enough to pull observations out of even quick sightings, and roomy enough for the long entries that special days deserve.
