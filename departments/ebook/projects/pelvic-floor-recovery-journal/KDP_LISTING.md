## Description (back cover / KDP A+)
Most postpartum care ends at the 6-week visit — but pelvic floor recovery doesn't. This 12-week daily journal is built for the fourth trimester, the months when leakage, prolapse heaviness, and perineal or C-section pain are most plastic — and most often dismissed.

Each daily page captures Kegel sets (long holds + quick flicks), leakage episodes, urge symptoms, prolapse heaviness, perineum / incision pain, sleep, mood, and movement. Weekly Reviews surface trends. Four Pelvic Floor PT Visit Prep pages turn three months of data into a clear conversation with your PT — most postpartum patients in the U.S. never get a referral, and showing up with data is how you change that.

What's inside:
- 12 weeks of daily symptom logs (0-5 severity scale, 8 core symptoms)
- Daily Kegel log: long holds, quick flicks, how it felt
- 12 weekly review pages with leakage trend graph
- 4 Pelvic Floor PT Visit Prep sheets (top symptoms, return-to-activity goals, questions, home program)
- Recovery milestones reference (week 1-2, 3-6, 6-week check-up, 7-12, beyond)
- Kegel & symptom guide (leakage types, prolapse sensations, pain locations, red flags)
- Baseline assessment (Day 1) + 12-week checkpoint
- Personal info, OB, PT, lactation consultant, and medication log

Designed for new moms after vaginal birth, C-section, or VBAC who want a real recovery, not a guesswork one — and who want to walk into their next pelvic-floor PT appointment prepared.
