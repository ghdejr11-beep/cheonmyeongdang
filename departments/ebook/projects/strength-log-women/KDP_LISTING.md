## Description
Stop training without a plan. **Track every lift. Build real strength.**

This 12-week strength training log is built for women who lift heavy — whether you're new to the squat rack, running a Bret Contreras program, or pushing through your fifth block of 5/3/1.

**What's inside (66 pages):**
- 48 daily workout pages (4 days/week × 12 weeks)
- Upper / Lower / Push / Pull split — works with any program
- 7 exercise rows per session, 5 sets each, with RPE
- 12 weekly review pages — wins, setbacks, next week's lift to push
- 12-Week PR Tracker — Squat, Bench, Deadlift, OHP, Hip Thrust + 5 more
- Body measurements grid (14 weeks)
- Starting stats + 12-week block review

**Designed for:**
- Women lifting 3-5x per week
- Anyone running a structured strength program (StrongCurves, 5/3/1, GZCLP, Glute Lab)
- Lifters who want to track progressive overload without an app

**Bonus columns:**
- Cycle Day tracker (because performance changes across the month)
- Sleep, energy, pre-workout fields
- Cardio / conditioning section (Hyrox, intervals, Zone 2)

Heavy paper-friendly format. No subscription. No app crashes. Just data on a page.
