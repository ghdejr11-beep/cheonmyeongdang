## Question (find/post on Quora):
> What is Saju (Four Pillars of Destiny) and what does it reveal?

## Answer (copy-paste):

**Saju is a traditional Korean fortune-telling system that maps your entire life's blueprint using your birth date and time.** The name literally means "four pillars" (사주), and it's one of the most deeply embedded forms of divination in Korean culture — consulted before marriages, career changes, even business launches.

## What Are the Four Pillars?

Each pillar represents a unit of time from your birth:

- **Year Pillar** — your ancestral roots, early childhood environment, and social reputation
- **Month Pillar** — your parents, education years, and core personality tendencies
- **Day Pillar** — your truest self and your relationship with a life partner
- **Hour Pillar** — your inner world, children, and the later years of life

Each pillar is built from two characters: a **Heavenly Stem** (천간) and an **Earthly Branch** (지지). These come from the ancient Chinese sexagenary cycle, giving you eight characters total — which is why Saju is also called **팔자 (Palja)**, meaning "eight characters."

## What Does It Actually Reveal?

A skilled Saju reader interprets the interplay of the **Five Elements** (wood, fire, earth, metal, water) and **yin-yang balance** within those eight characters. From this, they might discuss:

- **Natural strengths and weaknesses** in your personality
- **Career paths** that align with your elemental energy
- **Relationship compatibility** — this is huge in Korean dating culture
- **Decade-by-decade luck cycles** called 대운 (Daewoon), which shift every ten years

## Why Koreans Take It Seriously

Even highly educated, modern Koreans quietly consult a Saju reader before major life decisions. It's less about blind fate and more about **understanding your tendencies and timing**. Think of it like a detailed personality map that also factors in when favorable or difficult energy cycles arrive in your life.

My grandmother visited her Saju reader before every major family decision. She didn't treat the reading as absolute truth — more like a wise elder's perspective you weigh carefully.

## One Important Note

Saju readings vary enormously by the reader's skill and school of interpretation. Two readers can look at the same chart and emphasize different things. The system has real depth — serious practitioners study for years.

If you're curious to explore, **free online Saju calculators** can generate your basic eight characters, and **Korean culture ebooks or introductory astrology guides** often include beginner-friendly breakdowns of the Five Elements framework. From there, consulting an experienced reader gives you the full picture.
