## Answer (328 words)

The **Day Master (日干, 일간)** is the single Heavenly Stem at the top of your Day Pillar — and in Korean Saju it represents *you*. Everything else in your 8-character chart is interpreted relative to this one character.

If you only ever learn one thing about your Saju, learn your Day Master.

**Why it's structurally privileged**

There are 8 characters in your chart total. The Day Stem is privileged because:

- It's the character closest to *you* in time and space (your day of birth)
- All Ten Gods (십성) labels are calculated relative to it
- Your dominant strengths, blind spots, and natural roles are read from it directly
- Compatibility (궁합) starts with Day-Master-to-Day-Master comparison

It's roughly analogous to the Sun in Western astrology — but with 10 archetypes (5 elements × yang/yin polarity) instead of 12.

- **Yin Metal (신)** — Jewel, refined, polished beauty
- **Yang Water (임)** — Ocean, vast intelligence, deep flow
- **Yin Water (계)** — Stream, intuition, sustained gentleness

Each archetype has natural strengths, characteristic blind spots, and types of work it's structurally suited for.
