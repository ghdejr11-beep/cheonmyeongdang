## Answer (320 words)

The **8 characters (8자, "pal-ja")** are the raw structural data of your Saju chart. Everything else in Saju interpretation is derived from these 8.

**Where the 8 come from**

Your birth date, time, and place produce **4 pillars**, each containing **2 characters** (1 Heavenly Stem + 1 Earthly Branch). 4 pillars × 2 characters = 8 characters total.

The 4 pillars:

1. **Year Pillar** — derived from your birth year (Saju year transitions on Liknok/Lichun, around February 4, NOT January 1)
2. **Month Pillar** — derived from your birth month (also based on solar terms, not the Gregorian calendar)
3. **Day Pillar** — derived from your birth date (the top character of this pillar = your **Day Master**)
4. **Hour Pillar** — derived from your birth time (Saju uses 12 two-hour blocks, not 24 one-hour blocks)

If your birth time is unknown, you have **6 characters** instead of 8. Saju still works on 6 — you just can't read the late-life and hidden-self layers as deeply.

**The two character types**

- **Heavenly Stems (천간, 天干)** — 10 possible: 갑 을 병 정 무 기 경 신 임 계. These are the "tops" of each pillar and represent visible, expressed energy.
- **Earthly Branches (지지, 地支)** — 12 possible (the same 12 that produce the Chinese Zodiac animals): 자 축 인 묘 진 사 오 미 신 유 술 해. These are the "bottoms" of each pillar and represent hidden, structural energy.

**Each character has an element**

All 22 possible characters (10 stems + 12 branches) map to one of the Five Elements. So your 8 characters give you both an **element distribution** and a **stem-branch arrangement** to interpret.
