## Answer (314 words)

Reading your own Saju is like reading a 1,000-year-old language. You won't master it overnight, but here is the **5-step beginner's protocol** to extract 80% of the value in 20 minutes.

**Step 1 — Identify your Day Master (일간/Il-gan)**

Look at the **top character of the Day Pillar** (3rd column). This single character represents YOU. There are 10 possible Day Masters (Yang Wood, Yin Wood, Yang Fire, ... Yin Water).

- **Yang Wood (甲)**: tall pine tree — leader, principled, stubborn
- **Yin Wood (乙)**: vine — adaptive, social, persistent
- **Yang Fire (丙)**: sun — expressive, generous, volatile
- **Yin Fire (丁)**: candle — warm, focused, sensitive
- **Yang Earth (戊)**: mountain — stable, slow-moving
- **Yin Earth (己)**: field — nurturing, accommodating
- **Yang Metal (庚)**: sword — decisive, blunt
- **Yin Metal (辛)**: jewel — refined, sharp-tongued
- **Yang Water (壬)**: ocean — strategic, expansive
- **Yin Water (癸)**: rain — gentle, intuitive

**Step 2 — Count your Five Elements**

Tally how many of your 8 characters are Wood/Fire/Earth/Metal/Water. The element you have **least** is what your life will keep nudging you toward.

**Step 3 — Spot your dominant Ten God**

If your chart is loaded with **Wealth stars** → wealth is a life theme. **Officer stars** → career/status. **Resource stars** → learning, mentorship. **Output stars** → creativity, performance.

**Step 4 — Check your current 10-year cycle (대운)**

Your Daewoon table shows what energy is dominant in your life *right now*. A "Wealth decade" feels like financial focus; an "Officer decade" feels like career pressure. This explains why life *changes themes* every ~10 years.

**Step 5 — Identify one clash and one harmony**

Look for **冲 (clash)** and **合 (combine)** between your branches and the current year branch (2026 = 午). A clash year is upheaval; a combine year is alliance.
