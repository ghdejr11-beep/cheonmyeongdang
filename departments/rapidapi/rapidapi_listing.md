Get an authentic Korean Saju (also known as Bazi or the Four Pillars of Destiny) reading in under 3 seconds. Submit a solar-calendar birth date and gender; receive the four classical pillars (year/month/day/hour stems and branches in Hangul + Hanja), a complete five-element (Wood/Fire/Earth/Metal/Water) distribution, day-stem personality summary, lucky color, and lucky cardinal direction. Each call also returns a 3-sentence English advice paragraph generated by Claude, blending classical Korean metaphysics with practical modern second-person guidance for the current year.

Built on a production-grade Saju engine that has powered cheonmyeongdang.vercel.app and the Korean Cheonmyeongdang Android app: solar-to-lunar conversion, classical solar-term boundaries, 60-Ganji cycle, 10-year daeun calculation, and 12 yunseong life stages. No random outputs, no LLM-only guesses — algorithmic core with AI applied only to the final prose advice.

Perfect for astrology apps, dating apps, name-checking products, character-design tools, K-culture content sites, and personality quiz platforms targeting Korean, Chinese, Japanese, Vietnamese, and global Asian-culture audiences.
