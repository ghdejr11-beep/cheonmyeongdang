## Day 2 — "Five Elements 101" angle

**Post 2.1**
Korean Saju runs on 5 elements: Wood (목), Fire (화), Earth (토), Metal (금), Water (수).

Each one feeds another and beats another in a cycle:
• Wood feeds Fire (생)
• Fire makes Earth (ash)
• Earth births Metal
• Metal carries Water (condensation)
• Water grows Wood

That cycle = the whole system. 1/3

**Post 2.2**
Your birth date gets converted into 8 characters across the 4 pillars. Each character has an element.

Whichever element appears most → that's your dominant nature.
Whichever is missing → that's what you spend life seeking.

Two charts merge well when they fill each other's gaps. Crash when they reinforce excess.
