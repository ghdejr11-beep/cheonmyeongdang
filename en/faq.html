1. What is Saju (Four Pillars of Destiny / 四柱八字)?
Saju is the core concept of Korean Myeongli (命理) — your birth year, month, day, and hour each yield a Heavenly Stem (天干) and an Earthly Branch (地支), producing 8 characters total. These 8 characters reveal your personality, career path, relationships, wealth flow, and health tendencies through Yin-Yang and the Five Elements.

However, fortune interpretations are for reference only — never use as the sole basis for medical, legal, or financial decisions.
