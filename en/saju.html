FAQ

How is Saju different from Chinese Bazi?

They share roots in the same classical texts, but Korean Saju emphasizes the Day Master (일간) more heavily and uses Korean-specific interpretation lineages developed over the Joseon dynasty. The frameworks have diverged over 600+ years.

Is Saju like Indian Vedic astrology?

No — Vedic astrology is sidereal and planetary, Saju is calendrical and elemental. They answer different questions about the same person.

Do I need to believe in it?

No. Many Koreans treat Saju as a cultural framework for self-reflection, similar to MBTI in the West — useful regardless of metaphysical belief.

What if I don't know my exact birth time?

Provide what you have — many readings are insightful even without the hour. The day pillar (the core) is always available from just the date.
