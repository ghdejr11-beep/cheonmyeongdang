🍱 Korean Cooking 101

3 essential ingredients: Gochujang (red chili paste), Doenjang (soybean paste), Sesame oil. With these, you can recreate 80% of Korean dishes.

Banchan culture: Every Korean meal has 3-7 small side dishes (반찬). Most are no-cook fermented or pickled vegetables — meant to be made ahead.

Heat levels: Korean spice is layered, not just hot. Gochugaru (chili flakes) provides smoky base; gochujang adds sweet umami; fresh peppers add bite.
